Column schema
-------------
PTA_public_anchor_demo.csv:
  - freq_Hz : frequency in Hz
  - log10rho_median : median of log10 rho (demo units)
  - log10rho_p16    : 16th percentile (log10 rho)
  - log10rho_p84    : 84th percentile (log10 rho)

ESA_RCL2019_*_demo.csv:
  - f_Hz         : frequency in Hz
  - Sn_1_per_Hz  : one-sided strain noise PSD [1/Hz]
  - label        : text label for the curve
