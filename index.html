We model the universe as a 4‑D brane in a higher‑D bulk. The effective FRW equation acquires a high‑energy $\rho^2$ term and a dark‑radiation term. One parameter—the brane tension $\lambda$—links a predicted gravitational‑wave spectral break to an early‑universe radiation excess ($\Delta N_{\rm eff}$). That gives a single pass/fail rule across PTA→LISA and CMB/BBN.
