At a glance

  • Grand equation (flat FRW, k=0): modifies Friedmann with a high‑energy $\rho^2$ term and a dark‑radiation $\mathcal{C}/a^4$ term.
  • Single‑parameter falsifiability: one brane tension $\lambda$ fixes both the SGWB break frequency $f_\mathrm{br}(\lambda) \propto \lambda^{1/4}$ and the radiation excess $\Delta N_\mathrm{eff}$ via $\mathcal{C}$.
  • Low‑energy GR recovered: $\rho \ll \lambda \Rightarrow$ standard Einstein gravity (PPN/binary‑pulsar safe).
